CQC stands for Colombo Quality Camps. We are bunch of people got together to improve the quality awarness of the Sri Lankan quality assurance community. We organize events, meetups and sessions. We also do consultations for all your quality assurance needs.

Well, First of all we wanted to do something for the QA community. We identified that there are is a space for workshop in Quality Engineering area. So we started our journey in 2017 with our first quality camp which gained a huge success. From thta point onward we organize various sessions, meetups. Quality Week is our signature event which happened in every year.

Quality is week is our signature event. We organize it yearly basis to improve the knowledge of quality assurance community in Sri Lanka. We invite speakers from Sri Lanka and outside to speak in this glamorous event.
